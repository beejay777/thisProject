***Description/Theory***
* Filling algorithms 
* Boundary Fill Algorithm

***Inbuilt graphics functions***
* _floodfill()_ : floodfill(x, y, boundarycolor)
* _setfillstyle()_ : setfillstyle(pattern, color)
* _fillpoly()_ : fillpoly(no. of points in a polygon declared, points)
* _fillellipse()_ : fillellipse(x, y, xradius, yradius)
